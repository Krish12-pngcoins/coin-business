Our Authentication Process

Every coin passes a rigorous multi-stage verification before entering our inventory or being listed for sale.

1

Visual Examination

Initial inspection under 10x–40x magnification for die characteristics, surface originality, and signs of alteration.

2

Specific Gravity Test

Precious metal coins are weighed and measured for diameter/thickness. Density must match period specifications within 0.1%.

3

XRF Spectroscopy

Non-destructive X-ray fluorescence confirms metal composition. Identifies plating, fills, or non-period alloys immediately.

4

PCGS / NGC Submission

All significant coins are submitted to a major third-party grading service. We only sell raw coins below $150 face.

5

Provenance Research

For ancient and high-value coins, we trace collection history, auction records, and legal export compliance documentation.
